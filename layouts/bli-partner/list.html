Frequently Asked Questions

Is ReAI really free?

Yes, ReAI is completely free for accountants and firms. There are no hidden costs or limits on the number of clients.

How does ReAI make money?

We make money from other services and products, but the accounting system remains free for our partners.

Can I migrate existing clients?

Absolutely! We help you migrate data from existing systems at no cost. This includes all accounting data such as assets and other accounting records.

How long does the implementation take?

Most clients are up and running within 24 hours. Complex migrations can take a few days.
